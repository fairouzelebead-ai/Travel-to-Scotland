Travel to Scotland

Scotland is a country in Northern Europe, forming part of the United Kingdom, known for its dramatic landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture. With a population of about 5.5 million, it features a mix of rugged Highlands, over 790 islands, and historic cities like Edinburgh (capital) and Glasgow. Renowned for golf, whisky, and castles, it offers a distinct identity, with Gaelic and Scots spoken alongside English.
